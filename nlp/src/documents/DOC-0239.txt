### ONE Network Enterprises Marks 14th Year Under CEO Nguyen Anh Mai's Leadership

*Haven Island Special Economic Zone* — ONE Network Enterprises today commemorates fourteen years of transformative leadership under Chief Executive Officer Nguyen Anh Mai, who assumed the role in 63 PCE (2105 CE) and has since guided the company through an era of sustained growth, operational expansion, and meaningful civic investment across Clairos.

"Fourteen years ago, ONE Network made a commitment to the people and waterways of Clairos — to connect, to supply, and to serve," said a ONE corporate spokesperson on behalf of the executive team. "Under CEO Mai's leadership, that commitment has been honored and exceeded in every measurable respect."

---

**A Legacy of Growth on the Water**

Since CEO Mai took the helm in 63 PCE, ONE Network Enterprises has expanded its active fleet by 40%, strengthening the company's position as the backbone of global maritime logistics and deepening its presence across every major shipping corridor on Clairos. New vessel classes commissioned under her tenure have extended ONE's operational reach into previously underserved routes, delivering the resources that Haven and the wider world depend upon.

Her leadership style — consistently described within the company and across the industry as **visionary and action-oriented** — has defined ONE's strategic posture through periods of considerable global uncertainty. Where others hesitated, CEO Mai moved decisively. Where others managed, she built.

---

**The ONE Foundation for Maritime Healthcare**

Among the milestones that define her tenure, the establishment of the ONE Foundation for Maritime Healthcare stands as a testament to CEO Mai's conviction that a company of ONE's reach carries obligations beyond the commercial. Under her direct sponsorship and continued advocacy, the Foundation has funded **17 clinics across Clairos**, delivering healthcare access to port communities, maritime workers, and underserved island populations that have historically been beyond the reach of formal medical infrastructure.

The Foundation's work represents one of the largest sustained corporate healthcare philanthropic programs in the Haven Island Special Economic Zone, and its expansion continues to be a personal priority for CEO Mai.

---

**Looking Forward**

ONE Network Enterprises enters the next chapter of its story from a position of strength: expanded fleet capacity, deepened route networks, and a philanthropic footprint that reflects the company's values as much as its commercial success. No retirement announcement accompanies this milestone — CEO Mai remains firmly at the helm, and ONE Network's focus, as ever, is on the horizon ahead.
