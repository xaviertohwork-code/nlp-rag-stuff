**CGC Emerging Technologies Division — Director (72 PCE – present)**
Tsukamoto was appointed Director of the CGC Emerging Technologies Division in 72 PCE, following an open competitive selection process. The Division's mandate encompasses technical regulatory oversight of emerging and disruptive technologies operating within or affecting Haven's Special Economic Zone, including periodic review of compliance submissions from CGC-member corporations. He has held this directorship continuously since appointment and currently manages a division staff of twelve (12) full-time personnel.

During the 75 PCE internal review cycle, Tsukamoto formally recommended increasing the Division's audit staff from 12 to 20 full-time personnel, citing expanding regulatory scope. This recommendation remains under consideration by the HR Directorate and the CGC Budget Allocation Secretariat.
