## CYANITE INDUSTRIES MARKS COMPLETION OF NORTH HAVEN REACTOR COMPLEX UPGRADE
### Upgraded Complex Delivers 18% Higher Thermal Output, Reinforcing Floodwall Integrity Across Northern Haven

---

**NORTH HAVEN, HAVEN ISLAND** — Cyanite Industries is pleased to announce the successful completion of the North Haven Reactor Complex Upgrade, a landmark infrastructure project delivering an **18% increase in thermal output** to the nuclear fission reactor complex that serves as the primary power source for Haven's northern Floodwall network. The completion of this upgrade represents one of the most significant enhancements to Haven's core flood-defense infrastructure in over a decade.

The upgraded complex now supplies power to **Floodwall sections N-1 through N-7**, the continuous arc of northern defensive barriers that stand between Haven and the open ocean. These sections collectively bear the highest tidal load of any Floodwall segment, making reliable, high-capacity power generation a matter of direct consequence for every Havenite.

---

### Project Overview

The upgrade program commenced in the third quarter of 74 PCE and required **26 months to complete**, drawing on the full depth of Cyanite's engineering workforce. At peak staffing, the project employed **4,200 Cyanite engineers and technicians**, working across reactor core systems, coolant infrastructure, turbine assemblies, and integrated grid management architecture. Throughout the upgrade process, Floodwall operational continuity was maintained without interruption — a feat of logistical coordination that the project team considers among its most significant achievements.

### Statement from Director-General Takeshi Oyelaran

**Takeshi Oyelaran**, Director-General of Cyanite Industries, offered the following remarks on the project's completion:

> *"Every other megacorporation in Haven makes deals. We make the walls that keep the water out. This upgrade isn't a commercial milestone — it's a civic obligation fulfilled. When those pumps run at higher output, when those Floodwall sections hold against whatever the ocean sends our way, that is Cyanite Industries doing what we have always done: building the things that make everything else possible. The people of Haven don't need to know our name to benefit from our work, and that is precisely as it should be."*

---

### About Cyanite Industries

Cyanite Industries is a vertically integrated industrial enterprise engaged in mining, underwater resource extraction, heavy manufacturing, and construction operations across Clairos. Cyanite is the developer and primary maintenance authority for the Haven Island Floodwall system, including the nuclear fission reactor network and associated reverse osmosis water treatment infrastructure that serves Haven's population.
