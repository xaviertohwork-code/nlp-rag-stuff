## SECTION 1 — OPERATIONAL OVERVIEW

Operation Stillwater was a coordinated, pre-planned security action executed simultaneously across multiple Wampa Robotics facilities within the Haven Island Special Economic Zone. The operation was authorized by CGC Joint Security Resolution [CLASSIFIED], passed in executive session on 28-06-20 PCE.

All four remaining Original 5 megacorporations deployed contributing force contingents. Joint operational command was coordinated through the CGC Joint Operations Center. Each participating megacorporation maintained independent chain of command over its own units, with inter-contingent coordination managed through [CLASSIFIED] liaison officers embedded at each primary objective site.
