# CGC Issues Statement on Security Incident at Anniversary Ceremony

**Renhwa Media News Desk | 28-06-27 PCE | OPEN**

*Renhwa Media is a founding member of the Clairos Governance Council.*

---

**HAVEN** — The Clairos Governance Council issued a formal public statement on 28-06-27 PCE, two days after events at the CGC's 25th anniversary ceremony, characterizing what took place as a "security incident" and confirming that standard CGC operations have since resumed.

The statement, released through the CGC Secretariat's official communications channel, offered no additional elaboration beyond describing the incident as a contained matter of internal security. No individual corporations were named in connection with the incident, and no timeline was given for any further disclosure.

"The Clairos Governance Council confirms that a security incident occurred during proceedings at the 25th anniversary ceremony," the statement read in its opening clause. "The incident has been resolved. The Council thanks all members for their continued cooperation in maintaining the stability and integrity of the Haven Island Special Economic Zone."

---

**Wampa Robotics Ceases Operations**

Among the details confirmed in the CGC statement was a brief notice regarding Wampa Robotics, one of the five founding member corporations of the CGC. As of the date of the statement, Wampa Robotics has ceased operations. The statement did not describe the circumstances under which operations concluded, nor indicate whether any continuity of business activities is anticipated.

A CGC spokesperson, speaking on background, described the operational cessation as "consistent with the resolution of the security incident" and declined to provide further comment.

Wampa Robotics was among the Original 5 Firms at the CGC's founding in 3 PCE and had maintained active membership since that time. The CGC statement made no reference to whether Wampa Robotics' seat on the Council will be considered vacant or otherwise addressed under existing Council procedures.

---

**Ceremony Background**

The 25th anniversary ceremony was convened in Haven to mark a quarter-century of CGC governance of the Haven Island Special Economic Zone. Members of the Council and invited guests were in attendance. The CGC's statement confirmed the ceremony was underway at the time of the incident and did not characterize the incident as having disrupted the broader commemorative proceedings.

No further details regarding the ceremony's attendees, agenda, or conclusion were included in the official statement.

---

**No Further Statements Expected**

The CGC spokesperson indicated that no further public statements on the matter are expected in the near term, describing the incident as "closed." Member corporations of the CGC have not issued independent statements; this publication has not received responses to requests for comment submitted to individual member corporations as of press time.

This article reports the content of the CGC's official statement in full. Renhwa Media's editorial coverage reflects the information made available through official CGC communications channels. No independent account of the events at the anniversary ceremony has been verified by this publication, and no challenge to the Council's characterization of events has been raised through official channels. The CGC's description of a "security incident" stands as the complete public record at the time of publication.

Further updates will be reported as official information becomes available.
