### WE THE OPTED ANNOUNCES FOURTH ANNUAL MARCH FOR COGNITIVE LIBERTY
**Central Haven | 76-06-22 | Expected Attendance: 8,000**

---

We The Opted is proud to announce the fourth annual March for Cognitive Liberty, to be held on 76-06-22 in central Haven. Since the first March in 73 PCE, the event has grown into the most visible public demonstration for cognitive autonomy and megacorporate accountability in Haven. This year, we expect approximately 8,000 participants to join us in the streets.

"Every year, more Havenites find their way to us — Opted, curious, and everyone in between," said Mira Delacroix-Vey, founder of We The Opted. "This march is proof that the people of Haven have not surrendered their right to think freely. We will keep showing up until that right is recognized."

---

**EVENT DETAILS**

**Date:** 76-06-22
**Departure time:** 10:00
**Assembly point:** CGC Assembly Plaza, Central Haven

---

**ROUTE**

The march will depart from the CGC Assembly Plaza and proceed through central Haven along the civic corridor, concluding at the eastern sector boundary. Stewards will be posted at regular intervals throughout the route. Participants are encouraged to arrive at the Assembly Plaza no later than 09:30 for opening remarks.

We The Opted reminds all participants and the public that this is a peaceful demonstration. Marshals will be present along the full route to ensure that the march proceeds safely and without incident. We The Opted does not endorse and will not tolerate any conduct that undermines the peaceful character of this event.

---

**CONFIRMED SPEAKERS**

The following speakers are confirmed for the 76-06-22 programme:

- **Mira Delacroix-Vey**, Founder and Leader, We The Opted
- **Ada Oyelaran**, Senior Organizer, We The Opted

The full speaker programme, including additional confirmed voices from the Opted community and allied organizations, will be published no later than 76-06-15. We can confirm that **Jonas Ferretti is not among the confirmed speakers** for this year's march.

---

**ABOUT WE THE OPTED**

Founded in 73 PCE by Mira Delacroix-Vey, We The Opted is a humanist political advocacy organization representing Opted individuals and all those who oppose the unchecked influence of megacorporate power over daily life in Haven. We organize, we demonstrate, and we speak.
