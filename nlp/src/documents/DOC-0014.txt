## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report constitutes the final accounting of the Wampa Robotics asset redistribution process, initiated following the collective CGC security action of 28-09-28 (PCE). The Bureau hereby certifies the close of primary disposition proceedings, subject to the outstanding items enumerated in Appendix A.

The total estimated fair-market value of all seized Wampa Robotics assets, as assessed by the Bureau's independent valuation panels across all facility classes, stands at **14.7 billion Phi Credits**. This figure encompasses physical infrastructure, manufacturing equipment, vehicle and robotics inventory, intellectual property holdings, and liquid financial instruments held in Wampa's name at the time of seizure.

Final distribution to member corporations was completed on a proportional basis as determined by the CGC Member Allocation Framework agreed upon in session 28-11-03 (PCE). The patent portfolio declassification and licensing process, concluded in the first year following redistribution, generated **890 million Credits in first-year licensing revenue**, apportioned across all four recipient members in proportion to their primary allocations.

This report was finalized in the fifteenth month of year 30 PCE, approximately **18 months** after the initial asset seizure event of 28-09-28 (PCE). Seventeen (17) asset disposition items remain unresolved pending stabilization of former Wampa facilities in contested external territories; these items are documented in full in Appendix A and represent the sole outstanding matter before the Bureau.

The Bureau recommends archival of this report as the definitive instrument of record for the Wampa Robotics redistribution proceedings.

## SECTION 1 — VALUATION METHODOLOGY

All physical assets were assessed using replacement-cost methodology adjusted for operational depreciation, cross-referenced against pre-seizure Wampa financial disclosures filed with the CGC Secretariat. Intellectual property holdings were assessed using projected royalty stream modeling over a fifteen-year horizon at a 6.5% discount rate. Liquid instruments were recorded at face value as of the seizure date.

Three independent valuation panels were convened: one each nominated by the primary recipient members and a fourth nominated by the CGC Secretariat acting as a tiebreaker authority. Where panel valuations diverged by more than 3%, the Secretariat panel's figure was adopted. Disputed valuations totaling approximately 340 million Credits were resolved through this mechanism and are reflected in the figures below.

## SECTION 3 — PATENT PORTFOLIO AND LICENSING REVENUE

### 3.1 Portfolio Overview

The Wampa Robotics intellectual property estate, as catalogued by the Bureau's IP Division, comprised 1,847 active patents and 412 pending applications at the time of seizure. Of these, 1,203 patents were designated for full declassification and licensing pool entry; 644 were placed under member-exclusive licensing arrangements proportional to primary allocations; and the 412 pending applications were transferred to the respective member corporations' own IP portfolios according to their primary allocation percentages.

## SECTION 4 — OUTSTANDING MATTERS

As of the date of this report, primary disposition proceedings are complete and the Bureau certifies full accounting of the Φ 14.7 billion asset base. However, **seventeen (17) individual asset disposition items remain unresolved** due to ongoing guerrilla activity at former Wampa Robotics facilities located outside Haven. These items could not be safely assessed, transferred, or decommissioned within the primary proceedings window. Responsibility for each item remains in abeyance pending physical stabilization of the relevant site.

A full enumeration is provided in Appendix A. The Bureau notes that resolution of these items is not expected to materially alter the certified total valuation, as the combined estimated value of the outstanding items is assessed at no more than Φ 210 million under conservative assumptions.
