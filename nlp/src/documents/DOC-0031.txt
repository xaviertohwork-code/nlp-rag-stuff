RE: Renhwa Media Intelligence — Disposition Guidance

---

**I. Receipt Acknowledgement**

I have received and reviewed the intelligence briefing regarding suspected partnership activity between Renhwa Media and The Edge Corporation. No further circulation of that briefing is authorized beyond the distribution list of record.

---

**II. Situation Assessment**

The situation is potentially consequential but not yet actionable. I am not prepared to draw conclusions from what remains, at this stage, an incomplete picture. Partners are advised to treat the contents of the briefing accordingly — with interest, not urgency.

---

**III. Disposition**

Phyrexis will take no overt action on this intelligence. No deputy, no director, and no seconded operative is authorized to initiate any counter-intelligence response, active inquiry, or contact with any implicated party pending further guidance from this office. Continued passive monitoring only.

The rationale is straightforward: premature intervention could reveal the depth of our signals capability to parties who would benefit from knowing. That cost exceeds any near-term advantage intervention might yield.

---

**IV. Next Steps**

This office will determine when conditions warrant escalation. Until notified otherwise, Partners should consider this matter closed for purposes of any action.

— A.V.
