# ONE Network Enterprises
## INTERNAL DIRECTIVE — CONFIDENTIAL

**Document Reference:** ONE-DIR-77-0315-FM2
**Classification:** CONFIDENTIAL
**Date:** 77-03-15 PCE (2119-03-15 CE)
**From:** Nguyen Anh Mai, Chief Executive Officer, ONE Network Enterprises
**To:** Chief Operations Officer; Chief Financial Officer; Director of Fleet Acquisitions; Director of Capital Programs
**Subject:** Fleet Modernization — Phase II Authorization

---

### I. Authorization

Effective immediately, I authorize the release of **8,700,000,000 Phi Credits** in additional capital for Phase II of the ONE Network Enterprises Fleet Modernization Program. This allocation is approved unconditionally and is to be treated as a standing commitment for the purposes of all downstream procurement, contract, and scheduling actions.

Phase II scope is unambiguous: **twelve aging cargo vessels are to be replaced outright with newer automated hulls.** Refurbishment of existing assets is not authorized under this directive and does not constitute compliance with Phase II objectives. Any proposal from subordinate units suggesting a refurbishment path in lieu of replacement will be returned without review.

---

### II. Procurement Conditions

All Phase II vessel acquisitions must be sourced exclusively from **CGC-aligned shipbuilders**. No contract, letter of intent, framework agreement, or informal sourcing arrangement may be entered into with any shipbuilder operating outside CGC-aligned territory. The Director of Fleet Acquisitions will maintain an approved-builder registry updated no less than quarterly and will confirm alignment status prior to any procurement action. Deviations require my personal written approval.

---

### III. Delivery Timeline

Phase II deliveries are to be completed in full **no later than Q4 78 PCE**. I expect this deadline to function as a ceiling, not a target. The Director of Capital Programs is to establish quarterly milestone gates beginning Q2 77 PCE, with written status reports submitted to this office and to the CFO at the close of each quarter. Any shipbuilder agreement entered into under this directive must include contractual penalty provisions for delayed delivery consistent with ONE's standard fleet acquisition terms.

---

### IV. Phase I Context

For the record: Phase I of the Fleet Modernization Program, authorized in 76 PCE, carried a total capital allocation of **12,500,000,000 Phi Credits**. The Phase II authorization reflects a recalibrated scope focused on the remaining priority vessel cohort. Combined program investment positions ONE Network Enterprises to maintain operational superiority across its primary shipping corridors through the late 2120s.

---

### V. Execution

I expect the first Phase II procurement actions to be initiated within thirty days of this directive's circulation. Questions of interpretation are to be directed to the COO's office, not deferred. ONE does not wait.
