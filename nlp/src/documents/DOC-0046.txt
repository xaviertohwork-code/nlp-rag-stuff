**SUBJECT:** Emergency Trilateral Consultation Request — CGC Resolution 77-014

**FROM:** Federal Delegate, Nyari Confederation External Affairs Directorate
**TO:** Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Republic of Zonnon

---

**I. DELAY ACKNOWLEDGEMENT**

The Confederation Federal Council conveys its regrets for the considerable delay in formalising this request. As the Ministry will understand, achieving the supermajority consensus required to authorise an emergency consultation has taken forty-seven days from the date of initial deliberation. This duration reflects the constitutional realities of the Confederation's governance framework and is not, as we anticipate some in Zonnon's government may privately characterise it, a signal of diminished concern.

**II. ZONNON'S PROTEST FILING**

The Federal Council notes with some concern that the Republic of Zonnon filed an individual protest communication with the CGC Secretariat regarding Resolution 77-014 prior to any trilateral coordination. Whatever the merits of the filing's content, unilateral action of this nature risks presenting the Bloc to the CGC as a fragmented rather than unified body. The Confederation respectfully urges the Ministry to refrain from further uncoordinated filings pending the outcome of the proposed consultation. **It is Zonnon's filing that has created this exposure, not any action of the Confederation.**
