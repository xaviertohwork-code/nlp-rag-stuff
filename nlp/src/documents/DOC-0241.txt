## 1. Q4 76 PCE Performance

Sarento Production Division processed **2.1 million metric tonnes** of raw materials in Q4 76 PCE. This represents a **4% decline from Q3 76 PCE**, attributable in full to scheduled maintenance downtime across the primary ore processing lines during weeks 11 and 12 of the quarter. The maintenance window was planned and executed within the approved operational calendar; no unscheduled stoppages were recorded. Yield quality metrics held within acceptable tolerance bands throughout the quarter, and processing resumed at full capacity by the close of week 12.

Full-year 76 PCE aggregate figures are being compiled separately for the annual divisional report and will be forwarded to the Director-General's Office under separate cover.

---

## 2. Shipyard Output

Q4 shipyard completions comprised the following vessels, all produced against standing ONE Network Enterprises delivery orders:

- **2 × cargo haulers** (Class 7 heavy-lift configuration)
- **1 × patrol vessel** (coastal interdiction specification)

For the avoidance of any doubt in the delivery record: the quarter's output included **no frigates or warship-class vessels**. The three completed units — two cargo haulers and one patrol vessel — fulfill Q4 contractual obligations with ONE in full. Delivery transfer documentation has been forwarded to the Logistics & Supply Chain division.

The shipyards operated at 91% of rated throughput in Q4, consistent with the maintenance period referenced above. No structural delays are projected to carry into Q1 77 PCE.

---

## 3. Munitions Status

Munitions stockpile reserves at Sarento closed Q4 76 PCE at **142% of minimum required levels** across combined ordnance categories, including artillery, armoured vehicle munitions, and automated war unit resupply stores. Reserve accumulation has remained above the 130% floor for four consecutive quarters.

No draw-down orders were received in Q4. Current reserve trajectory supports the 77 PCE target posture without additional production uplift unless an operational deployment request is received from the Director-General's Office or Operations Command.

---

## 4. 77 PCE Production Targets

The approved **77 PCE annual production target** for raw material processing is set at **37 million metric tonnes**. Achieving this figure requires an average quarterly throughput of approximately 9.25 million metric tonnes, which represents a step-up from achieved 76 PCE rates. Division operations management has reviewed shift scheduling and capital equipment availability and confirms the target is achievable assuming no unplanned equipment failures and continued timely receipt of extraction feedstock via ONE shipping contracts.

Shipyard and munitions production targets for 77 PCE will be circulated under the Q1 production planning schedule following confirmation of ONE delivery orders for the year.

---

## 5. Facility Expansion Notes

Planning is proceeding for the Phase 2 expansion of the Sarento eastern processing corridor. Surveys of the Sarento zone have confirmed that **2 of the 6 former launch sites** within the zone footprint require full demolition to make way for the planned expansion footprint. The remaining sites fall outside the expansion corridor and require no immediate action.

Demolition scheduling for the two identified sites is being coordinated with the Sarento site management team. Provisional clearance is targeted for Q2 77 PCE to avoid overlap with the peak extraction feedstock intake window in Q3.

No regulatory obstacles to demolition are currently flagged.
