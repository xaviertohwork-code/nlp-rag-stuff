Effective this date, I authorize a three-person internal team, hereafter designated **Project Crescendo**, to conduct confidential partnership exploration with CGC-member technology companies. The following directives govern this initiative without exception.

---

**DIRECTIVE 1 — SCOPE**
Crescendo is authorized to explore partnership structures with CGC-member technology companies exclusively. No exploration of partnerships outside CGC membership is authorized under this directive. Any proposed deviation requires written authorization from the CEO prior to action.

**DIRECTIVE 2 — COMMUNICATIONS SECURITY**
All Project Crescendo communications — without exception — are to be conducted outside standard Renhwa corporate channels. Use of standard Renhwa messaging infrastructure, email routing, or document management systems for any Crescendo-related correspondence is expressly prohibited. The team lead is responsible for establishing and maintaining compliant out-of-channel communications architecture prior to any substantive exploration activity.

**DIRECTIVE 3 — DOCUMENTATION DISCIPLINE**
No written reference to specific company names shall appear in any Project Crescendo documentation, internal or otherwise. Counterparties are to be referenced by internally assigned designators only. Any document found in violation of this restriction will be treated as a security failure and escalated directly to the CEO.

**DIRECTIVE 4 — PERSONNEL**
Project Crescendo membership is fixed at the three individuals named in the sealed distribution record attached to this directive. No additions, substitutions, or expansions of team membership are authorized without direct CEO approval. Project Crescendo personnel are not to disclose their participation in this initiative to any other Renhwa employee, including members of the executive leadership team not already party to this memo.

**DIRECTIVE 5 — REPORTING**
The team lead will report directly and exclusively to the CEO. No reporting through intermediary organizational structures. Reporting cadence and format to be agreed verbally.

**DIRECTIVE 6 — CONTAINMENT**
Any indication that Crescendo's existence or scope has become known outside the authorized distribution list is to be reported to the CEO immediately. Crescendo is to be treated as the most operationally sensitive initiative currently active within this company. Act accordingly.

---

This authorization is valid for **180 days from the date of this directive**. At the conclusion of this period, all activity ceases unless the CEO issues written renewal. No grandfathering of ongoing discussions beyond the authorization window.

No questions in writing.

— PSH
