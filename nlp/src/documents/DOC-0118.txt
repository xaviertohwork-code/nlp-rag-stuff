# HAVEN YOUTH ATHLETICS PROGRAMME
## Official Press Release

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
**Date: 77-03-01**
**Classification: OPEN**

---

### Registration Now Open for the 77 PCE Summer Season

The Haven Youth Athletics Programme (HYAP) is thrilled to announce that the 77 PCE Summer Season is just around the corner — and we want every young Havenite in the mix.

HYAP exists to keep youth aged 8 to 17 active, connected, and competing across all five Haven sectors: North Haven, East Haven, West Haven, South Haven, and Central Haven. Whether your child is a seasoned competitor or lacing up for the very first time, there is a place for them on one of our teams.

---

### Registration Dates

Please note that registration does **not** open today. The 77 PCE Summer Season registration window **opens on 77-03-15** and **closes on 77-04-30**. Families are encouraged to register early, as popular disciplines fill quickly. Registration is completed through your sector's HYAP coordination office or via the HYAP community notice boards at all Havenite civic service centers.

---

### Sport Disciplines on Offer

This season, HYAP is proud to offer **12 sport disciplines** for participants to choose from, including:

- **Aquatic Relay** — team-based competitive swimming in Haven's enclosed circuit pools
- **Sprint Cycling** — short-course velocity racing on HYAP's dedicated track facilities
- **Vertical Climbing** — structural ascent competition on purpose-built indoor walls
- **Tide Rowing** — coordinated long-boat rowing across Haven's inner harbour channels
- **Pressball** — a fast-paced team sport played with a pressurized rebound sphere
- **Deck Athletics** — a multi-event track-and-field format adapted for Haven's elevated sport platforms
- **Weighted Circuit** — strength and endurance combined format for older age brackets
- **Drift Sailing** — introductory sailing within Haven's protected coastal enclosures

…and four additional disciplines announced at sector-level registration events. Check with your local HYAP coordinator for the full listing.

---

### Programme Fees and Subsidies

Participation in the HYAP Summer Season is **subsidized at 25 Phi Credits per season per participant** — well below the operational cost of running the programme, thanks to civic funding and in-kind contributions from Haven's community partners.

We believe that no young person should be kept off the field by financial circumstance. **Full fee waivers are available for low-income families.** Waiver applications are handled confidentially by HYAP coordinators at each sector office. Families are encouraged to apply at the time of registration; documentation requirements are kept as simple as possible.

---

### Last Season's Results

The 76 PCE season was our strongest to date. HYAP enrolled **18,400 participants** across all five sectors — a figure that reflects the extraordinary energy and commitment of Haven's young people, their families, and our volunteer coaching staff. We are determined to surpass that number in 77 PCE.
