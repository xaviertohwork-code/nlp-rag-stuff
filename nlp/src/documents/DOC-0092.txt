## INTERNAL MEMORANDUM

**CLASSIFICATION: YELLOW**

**TO:** Regional Spokespeople, Media Liaison Officers, External Affairs Representatives

**RE:** Floodwall Political Messaging — 63 PCE Media Cycle Preparation

---

### BACKGROUND

This memorandum is issued in direct response to the Renhwa Media anniversary article published in 62 PCE, which has renewed external commentary regarding Cyanite's role in Floodwall operations and what certain outlets are characterizing as institutional leverage over Haven's governance structures. The article has generated sufficient pickup that we expect follow-on requests and adversarial interview angles to intensify heading into the 63 PCE media cycle.

All spokespeople are to treat this as an active guidance document effective immediately.

---

### APPROVED TALKING POINTS

All public-facing communications on Floodwall matters must anchor to the following approved statements. Do not improvise. Do not accept the framing of hostile questioners. Return to these lines:

- **"Cyanite keeps Haven dry — that is not leverage, it is responsibility."**
- Haven's infrastructure requires sustained, long-term investment that no other entity on Clairos has been willing or able to provide. Cyanite stepped forward. That commitment is ongoing.
- Characterizing essential civil infrastructure management as political power consolidation is reckless politicization of public safety. Havenites deserve better than that from their media.
- Cyanite operates under full CGC oversight. The suggestion that our role constitutes undue influence fundamentally misunderstands how the Governance Council functions.

---

### PROHIBITED ENGAGEMENT

Under no circumstances should any spokesperson engage with, repeat, define, or respond to the phrase **"veto that needs no vote."** Do not quote it. Do not dispute it on its terms. Do not acknowledge it as a meaningful frame. Treat it as a non-question and redirect immediately to approved talking points. Any spokesperson who is caught engaging with this phrase on the record will be subject to immediate communications review.

If pressed, the approved deflection is: *"I'm not going to dignify politically motivated framing that puts Haven's residents at risk."*

---

### RECOMMENDED PROACTIVE ACTIONS

The following actions are recommended for execution in Q1 of 63 PCE:

1. **Commission independent engineering assessment.** We recommend engaging a credentialed third-party engineering body to produce a published assessment confirming that Cyanite's cumulative Floodwall investment levels exceed CGC Levy contributions by a factor of three. This provides an external, defensible citation for spokespeople and neutralizes the "cost-shifting" narrative before it gains wider traction.

2. **Proactive op-ed placement.** Draft and place a Cyanite-bylined piece in non-Renhwa outlets emphasizing the civic nature of Floodwall stewardship. Focus on continuity and reliability. Do not be defensive.

3. **Pre-brief friendly CGC contacts.** Before any CGC session in Q1, ensure aligned member representatives are aware of our messaging position. We do not want to be caught flat-footed if this surfaces in chamber.

4. **Monitor secondary pickup.** Track which outlets are running derivative coverage from the Renhwa piece. Escalate to this office if any pickup occurs in outlets with Sharpsea Bloc distribution.
