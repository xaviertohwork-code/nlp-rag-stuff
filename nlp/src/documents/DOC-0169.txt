## SECTION 6: PERFORMANCE NOTES

Annual performance evaluations on file covering 73 PCE through 76 PCE (2115–2118 CE) are uniformly consistent in their assessments. Reviewers commend Director Carstens's analytical thoroughness, the reliability of her compliance documentation, and her maintenance of productive working relationships with member corporation representatives.

Evaluations from 74 PCE (2116 CE) onward, however, flag a recurring pattern of concern warranting supervisory attention: **Director Carstens demonstrates a tendency to prioritise stakeholder accommodation over enforcement timelines**, frequently recommending deadline extensions in lieu of formal enforcement actions when member corporations signal resistance to compliance requirements. Reviewing officers note that while individual extension recommendations are often defensible in isolation, the cumulative pattern across multiple resolution cycles has in several instances resulted in delayed implementation of CGC Secretariat directives.

The 76 PCE (2118 CE) evaluation recommends that Director Carstens develop and present, by mid-77 PCE, a revised internal framework for distinguishing between extensions warranted by genuine operational constraint and those reflecting deference to member corporation preferences. This action item remains open as of the date of this record.
