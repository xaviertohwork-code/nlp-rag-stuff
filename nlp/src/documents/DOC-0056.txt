**2118-09-02**

**0342** — Passive sonar contact detected, bearing 074, range approx. 6.2 nautical miles. Contact designated ROMEO-17. Profile inconsistent with registered aquaculture maintenance traffic for this sector. Speed estimate 14 knots, heading 221. Logging and monitoring initiated.

**0347** — Attempted radio contact on standard maritime frequencies, Channels 16 and 11. No response from ROMEO-17. Second hail transmitted. No response. Contact maintaining course and speed.

**0352** — Third radio hail attempted. No response received. Contact's failure to respond to standard maritime frequencies noted as grounds for active pursuit. Helmsman directed to intercept course. Engines advanced to pursuit speed.

**0412** — Pursuit underway. ZMD-118 speed 22 knots. ROMEO-17 has altered course slightly to 204, speed increased to estimated 16 knots. All watch personnel at stations. Situation report transmitted to Eastern Sector Command.

**0528** — Interception achieved. Pursuit duration: 106 minutes. Maximum speed maintained by ZMD-118: 22 knots. ROMEO-17 brought to alongside under verbal orders via loudhailer. Vessel is an unmarked mid-range survey-type hull, approximately 28 meters, no visible registration on port side. Crew directed to prepare for boarding.

**BOARDING RECORD — 2118-09-02, 0541**

**Crew Identification:**
Six individuals aboard. All crew members presented identification documenting affiliation with Deepscan Maritime Services. Documents examined and photographed. Crew cooperative following initial reluctance to open forward compartment. No weapons declared or located. All six individuals detained aboard pending sector command instruction.
