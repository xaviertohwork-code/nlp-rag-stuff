## RENHWA MEDIA ANNOUNCES STUDIO PRISM: A DEDICATED IMMERSIVE CONTENT PRODUCTION FACILITY IN EAST HAVEN

**HAVEN** — Renhwa Media today announced the grand opening of **Studio Prism**, a state-of-the-art immersive content production facility located in east Haven. Representing an investment of **85 million Phi Credits**, Studio Prism marks the most significant single capital commitment in the company's recent history and signals Renhwa Media's conviction that immersive content represents the defining frontier of entertainment for the next generation of audiences.

Studio Prism is housed within Renhwa's east Haven offices, making productive use of previously downsized space through an extensive interior renovation. The facility has been purpose-built from the ground up to house the full production pipeline required for immersive content creation — from pre-production and spatial audio engineering to final delivery and distribution mastering.

---

### A TEAM BUILT FOR THE FUTURE

At launch, Studio Prism will employ approximately **300 dedicated immersive content specialists** — writers, directors, experiential designers, and technical producers recruited from across the Clairos entertainment and technology industries. Combined with the studio's 85 million Phi Credits investment base, the facility represents an unambiguous statement about the scale of Renhwa Media's ambitions in the immersive space.

---

### PLATFORM-AGNOSTIC BY DESIGN

Studio Prism is engineered to produce content compatible with **all major immersive platforms**, a deliberate architectural and technical choice. Renhwa Media believes that audience reach, not platform exclusivity, is the defining success metric for immersive entertainment — and Studio Prism's production pipeline reflects that belief at every stage.

### LEADERSHIP STATEMENT

**Park Soo-Hyun**, Chief Executive Officer of Renhwa Media, offered the following remarks at today's ribbon-cutting ceremony at the east Haven facility:

> *"Studio Prism is the physical embodiment of Renhwa's future. Every square meter of this facility exists to remind us — and the world — what Renhwa Media is capable of when we commit fully to something. We are not hedging. We are building."*
