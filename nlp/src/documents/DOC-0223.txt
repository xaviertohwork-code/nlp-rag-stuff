### Genesis Labs Announces Expanded Somatic Clinic Hours and Updated Treatment Protocols for 77 PCE

**East Haven, Haven Island** — Genesis Labs today announces that its East Haven Somatic Clinic will extend its operating hours to **18 hours per day**, effective **77-02-01**, as part of a continued commitment to making safe, clinically supervised Somatic Augmentation accessible to all Havenites. The expansion reflects record demand for Genesis Labs' industry-leading treatment services and underscores the company's position as the responsible standard-bearer in Somatic care across Clairos.

---

#### Expanded Access for a Growing Community

The East Haven Somatic Clinic currently processes an average of **340 Somatic Augmentation treatments per week**, a volume that reflects both the breadth of Havenites seeking augmentation services and the trust they place in Genesis Labs' clinical infrastructure. The extended hours will allow the clinic to serve a greater number of patients across all shifts, reducing wait times and improving scheduling flexibility for those whose work and life commitments make daytime appointments difficult.

"Every patient who walks through our doors receives a treatment tailored to their individual genome," said Cary Mullin, Founder, Chief Science Officer, and Chief Executive Officer of Genesis Labs. "That commitment does not change as we scale — it deepens. Our expanded hours are a reflection of our belief that clinical precision and broad access are not in tension. They are obligations we hold simultaneously."

---

#### Updated Treatment Protocols: The Second-Generation Standard

In conjunction with the expanded hours announcement, Genesis Labs confirms updated treatment protocols formalising the primacy of **second-generation biological growth enhancers** across all clinical procedures at its East Haven facility. Second-generation enhancers achieve full Somatic conversion with a superior tolerability profile, reduced side-effect incidence, and a more gradual, closely monitored integration process — outcomes that Genesis Labs' clinical teams have documented extensively since the second generation became the clinical standard following its development in 2084.

First-generation biological growth enhancers remain in Genesis Labs' inventory exclusively under **strict regulatory storage protocols**, in full compliance with applicable CGC biosafety guidelines. They are not part of the standard course of treatment administered at any Genesis Labs Somatic Clinic.

---

#### A Note on Patient Safety

Genesis Labs takes this opportunity to remind the public of the serious health risks associated with unregulated first-generation enhancers — commonly known as **Gleam** — obtained through street-level biodealers. These substances are dispensed without clinical oversight, without genome compatibility assessment, and without any of the quality controls that a licensed Somatic Clinic provides. Genesis Labs has observed cases in which unsupervised use of such products has resulted in adverse outcomes that our clinical teams are subsequently asked to address.

We express this not to alarm, but to protect. The path to safe augmentation runs through qualified clinical supervision. Havenites deserve nothing less.
