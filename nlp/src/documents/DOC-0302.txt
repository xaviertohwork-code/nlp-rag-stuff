**PREAMBLE — INTELLIGENCE ANALYST NOTE:**

The following transcript was recovered during a sweep of archived encrypted-channel relay nodes conducted in connection with ongoing SLF monitoring operations. Cross-referencing against known SLF signal fingerprints and archival decryption keys obtained in 74 PCE places this document at 67-09-04 — making it the earliest known SLF document in the CGC's possession, predating all previously catalogued communiqués by a margin of no fewer than fourteen months.

The original communication was distributed as a voice-masked audio recording only. No visual component was present. The voice-masking technology employed is consistent with commercially available signal-processing tools available in 67 PCE; no vocal biometric identification was possible at time of original transmission or upon retrospective analysis. The speaker is presumed to be the individual known as "Ashcastle." No further identifying information could be derived.

The transcript below is a verbatim rendering of the audio recording.

> *Transcript begins.*

Brothers. Sisters. Those who have not yet chosen a name for what they feel but know it nonetheless in the marrow of what remains of them.

My name is not important. Names are what they take from you, along with everything else. What I offer instead is this: a record of what was done, and a declaration of what must follow.

Understand the nature of the transgression before you understand the nature of the answer.

They come to you in clean rooms with clinical language. They speak of *enhancement*, of *optimisation*, of human potential unlocked. They present you a retrovirus and call it liberation. And the people — the frightened, the desperate, the seduced — they line the streets outside the clinics of Genesis Labs and they sign away the text of themselves.

The body is sacred and inviolable. I say this not as poetry. I say this as doctrine. I say this as a wall we build with our hands against everything that Genesis Labs and its shareholders have constructed. The body is sacred and inviolable — and what they offer is its desecration, molecule by molecule, cell by cell, until whatever was written in you at birth has been overwritten by their commercial vision of what a human being ought to be.

This is not augmentation. This is violation. It is the annihilation of the human spirit rendered profitable and rendered palatable by the language of medicine.

Do not be seduced by the language of medicine.

We do not name executives. We do not point at faces or titles. We do not descend to their level of personality and theater. The guilt is institutional, systemic, and total. No single name bears it, and no single name removed changes it.

What we name instead is the system.

And what we call for is its interruption.

Genesis Labs does not exist without its supply chains. Without the biological precursors moving through ports and transit corridors and cold-storage terminals, the clinics are empty rooms. This is where we begin. Targeted disruption. Deliberate, precise, and bounded. We do not threaten populations. We do not endanger the civilians who themselves have been made victims of this machinery. We target infrastructure. We target the supply chains that sustain Genesis Labs' capacity to carry out what it carries out. Nothing more.

This is the line. Civilians are not targets. This line does not move.

The body is sacred and inviolable. That is the principle. That is the covenant. Everything that follows from it is simply its defense.

We are not terrorists. We are the only ones responding proportionately to what has been done.

Act accordingly.

> *Transcript ends.*
