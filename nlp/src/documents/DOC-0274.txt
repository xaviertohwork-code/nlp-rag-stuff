### SECTION II — BACKGROUND

Sim Jiahong is the founding executive of the organisation now designated The Edge Corporation, having led the entity under its former name, Edge Research, from its inception as an independent legal entity. He was not appointed to this role by a board or external authority; he is the company's founder, and his authority derives from that founding position rather than from any governance body external to the organisation. His tenure therefore predates the independent legal incorporation event of 40 PCE, tracing its origins to the earlier joint venture structure created in 25 PCE (2067 CE) by Phyrexis Group and Cyanite Industries.

The corporate renaming from Edge Research to The Edge Corporation, formalised on the date of this dossier's compilation, follows Phyrexis Group's public severing of its funding relationship with the organisation in 68 PCE (2110 CE), itself a consequence of the disruption caused by the commercial launch of the Edge platform. The renaming is assessed as an exercise in brand independence rather than any material change in the subject's executive authority or strategic orientation.

### SECTION III — PROFESSIONAL HISTORY

Sim Jiahong's operational record spans the entirety of Edge Research's development as an independent technology venture. Principal milestones relevant to CGC tracking:

- **25 PCE (2067 CE):** Edge Research established as a joint venture of Phyrexis Group and Cyanite Industries. Sim Jiahong assumes leadership from formation.
- **41 PCE (2083 CE):** Phyrexis and Cyanite formally spin Edge Research out as an independent legal entity. Subject retains executive authority without interruption.
- **68 PCE (2110 CE):** Edge platform launched across Haven. Phyrexis publicly severs funding relationship.
- **69 PCE (2111 CE):** Edge Research formally redesignated The Edge Corporation. Subject's title and authority unchanged.

No prior employment with CGC member organisations on record. No formal academic qualifications on file with the Personnel Division; subject's technical expertise appears to be substantially self-developed within the Edge Research environment.

### SECTION IV — PERSONALITY ASSESSMENT

Subject is assessed as **sharp, well-spoken, and unafraid to publicly disagree with the Old 3 Firms** in CGC-adjacent settings and industry forums. This tendency is not assessed as incidental or reactive; it appears to reflect a deliberate rhetorical posture designed to position The Edge Corporation as a disruptive challenger to established CGC interests. His public statements regarding the Old 3 are consistently critical in substance while remaining within the register of competitive corporate discourse rather than inflammatory polemic. Personnel Division assesses this pattern as likely to intensify if the organisation approaches accession thresholds.

Subject is noted for minimal social friction in structured settings — meetings, prepared remarks — and for an absence of the discursive tangents observed in some other high-profile industry figures. He is economical in communication, which tends to make his criticisms of Old 3 positions land with greater precision than volume.

### SECTION V — PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION AND PERSONAL HABITS

Subject is instantly recognisable at all public appearances for a **uniform-like wardrobe of identical dark suits**, worn without observable variation across press engagements, industry events, and documented semi-formal settings. He has addressed this practice directly in prior media contexts, characterising it publicly as **a productivity decision** — eliminating daily wardrobe deliberation in favour of cognitive resources directed at operational priorities.

Personnel Division notes for completeness that subject is known to maintain **exactly four sets of the same suit** to ensure consistency across all public appearances, accounting for garment maintenance and travel scheduling without requiring any deviation from the established presentation. This detail is assessed as illustrative of a precise, systematised approach to personal brand management that extends into his broader operational style.
