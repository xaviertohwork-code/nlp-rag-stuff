## CYANITE INDUSTRIES COMPLETES NORTH HAVEN GRID SUBSTATION 4 UPGRADE TWO WEEKS AHEAD OF SCHEDULE

**NORTH HAVEN** — Cyanite Industries today announces the successful completion of the North Haven Grid Substation 4 upgrade, delivered on 76-11-18 — two weeks ahead of the project's original scheduled completion date.

The upgraded Substation 4 now delivers a total power throughput capacity of 820 megawatts, representing a 14% increase over the previous installation. This figure reflects the final commissioned output following full load-testing and verification by Cyanite's infrastructure engineering teams.

---

### PROJECT FINANCIALS

The upgrade was funded entirely by Cyanite Industries at a total project cost of 47 million Credits. No external financing, CGC grants, or third-party contributions were sought or accepted. Cyanite underwrote the full scope of design, procurement, civil works, and commissioning in-house.

---

### INFRASTRUCTURE SERVICES

The upgraded Substation 4 serves a dual operational mandate within north Haven. It provides primary power supply to Cyanite's north Haven logistics hub — one of the company's principal distribution and staging facilities on Haven Island — and additionally supplies three of the four northern Floodwall pumping stations. The pumping stations draw on Substation 4's capacity to support the continuous drainage operations that maintain Haven Island's flood protection integrity.

### ABOUT CYANITE INDUSTRIES

Cyanite Industries is Clairos's foremost vertically integrated mining, extraction, heavy manufacturing, and construction company. As the developer and sole maintainer of Haven Island's Floodwall system, Cyanite provides the essential physical foundations upon which Haven's 100 million residents depend. Cyanite's operational footprint spans north Haven, its primary industrial center at Sarento, and extensive resource extraction sites across Clairos. Cyanite Industries is a founding member of the Clairos Governance Council and one of the Original 5 Firms.
