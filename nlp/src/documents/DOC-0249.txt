## Genesis Labs Announces Expansion of Somatic Clinic Network: Three New Facilities Across Haven

**EAST HAVEN, HAVEN ISLAND** — Genesis Labs today announced the opening of three new Somatic Clinics across Haven Island, marking the company's most significant single expansion of its Havenite clinical footprint to date. New facilities are opening in central Haven, north Haven, and east Haven. With the addition of these three clinics, Genesis Labs now operates **nine Somatic Clinics** across Haven Island, bringing personalized Somatic Augmentation services closer to communities across the city.

Each new clinic is fully equipped to deliver Genesis Labs' complete suite of personalized Augmentation services, including individual genome sequencing and custom retrovirus tailoring — the standard of care that Genesis Labs has pioneered to minimize off-target recombination risk and maximize treatment efficacy. All three facilities will exclusively use Genesis Labs' second-generation biological growth enhancers, which the company describes as safer, better tolerated, and producing fewer adverse effects than first-generation alternatives.

Genesis Labs Founder, Chief Science Officer, and Chief Executive Officer Cary Mullin offered the following statement:

> "What makes this expansion genuinely exciting to me — and I'll admit I have to stop myself from going into significant depth here about retroviral vector distribution across somatic cell populations, because there is a truly remarkable story in that data — is that we are not simply adding square footage. We are putting precision genomic medicine within reach of communities in Haven who have historically had to weigh access against cost and distance. By the end of 77 PCE, this expansion will make personalized Somatic Augmentation accessible to 40% more Havenites. That is the number I keep coming back to. Forty percent."

The three new clinics are expected to be fully operational within 30 days of today's announcement.

Genesis Labs' Somatic Clinics offer a medically supervised alternative to unsupervised or street-sourced Augmentation products, with individualized care pathways designed to the specific genome of each patient. Prospective patients are encouraged to schedule an initial consultation through the Genesis Labs clinical portal.

The new Haven facilities join Genesis Labs' broader global network of Somatic Clinics. The company's largest Somatic Clinic outside Haven remains its flagship Tavenport facility, located on Zonnon Island in the Republic of Zonnon, which continues to serve as Genesis Labs' primary external research and clinical hub.
