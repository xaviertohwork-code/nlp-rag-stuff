**DAY 1 — 2117-07-31**
Departed Tavenport 0600. Eastern Sharpsea patrol corridor established per standing orders. Sea conditions Force 6; heavy swell from the northeast, deck operations limited to essential personnel. No contacts logged.

**DAY 2 — 2117-08-01**
Continued eastern corridor sweep. Sea conditions Force 6, persisting; whitecapping throughout, vessel taking moderate roll. No vessels of interest observed. All crew and systems nominal.

**DAY 3 — 2117-08-02**
Sea conditions eased to Force 4 as storm system tracked northeast. During mid-morning sweep, radar contact established with unidentified vessel bearing 047, range approximately 9 nautical miles. Hailing attempted on channels 16 and 12; no response received. ZMD-Osprey moved to intercept. Unidentified vessel altered course and departed the area at speed before interception was possible. No hostile engagement occurred. Contact logged for intelligence dissemination.

**DAY 4 — 2117-08-03**
Sea conditions Force 4; moderate seas, visibility good. Continued eastern corridor coverage. No contacts of interest. Routine equipment checks completed; all systems serviceable.

**DAY 5 — 2117-08-04**
Sea conditions Force 4. Full patrol sweep of northeastern boundary of assigned area. No vessels detected. No hostile engagement occurred on this date or any date during this patrol period. Crew welfare nominal.

**DAY 6 — 2117-08-05**
Sea conditions Force 4, slight improvement in swell period. Completed transit of eastern marker buoy line. No contacts logged. Minor chart update transmitted to ZMDC regional command.

**DAY 7 — 2117-08-06**
Sea conditions Force 4. Return transit to Tavenport initiated 0800. All patrol obligations fulfilled. Vessel and crew in good order upon return to port.

---

**WEEK 31 SUMMARY**

Patrol area: Eastern Sharpsea. Duration: 7 days.

Sea conditions summary: Force 6 (Days 1–2); Force 4 (Days 3–7).

Contacts of interest: One (1) unidentified vessel, Day 3. Vessel failed to respond to hailing on standard channels and vacated the area before ZMD-Osprey could close to identification range. Contact details forwarded to ZMDC intelligence section per standing procedure.

Hostile engagements: None. No hostile engagements occurred at any point during the patrol week.

Materiel status: No damage. No expenditures of ordnance or consumables beyond routine operational use.
