### INCIDENT REPORTS

**INCIDENT NHS-77-0214**
**Date/Time:** 77-02-05, approx. 08:40
**Location:** Block N-6, Lot 22
**Category:** Illegal Dumping — Hazardous Material

Collection operative Crew 7 (route N-6-C) discovered approximately 12–15 sealed industrial drums during standard kerbside pass. Drums bore no manifest markings; faint chemical odour noted. Crew 7 did not handle drums; area cordoned and supervisor notified at 08:52. Environmental assessment unit contacted 09:10. Drums confirmed to contain unidentified chemical waste. Matter referred same day to CGC Environmental Compliance for investigation and collection by specialist unit. North Haven Sanitation assumes no further custodial responsibility pending CGC Compliance response.

**Note for record:** Discovery date is **77-02-05**. Log entry time-stamped accordingly.

---

**INCIDENT NHS-77-0219**
**Date/Time:** 77-02-07, approx. 14:15
**Location:** Transit corridor N-2, junction with Industrial Spur 4
**Category:** Vehicle Collision

Collection vehicle **NHV-09** (route N-2-B, afternoon run) was involved in a low-speed collision with an automated freight cart travelling Industrial Spur 4. Automated cart failed to yield at junction; NHV-09 operator applied emergency braking; contact occurred. **No personnel injuries reported.** NHV-09 sustained damage to front nearside panel and refuse compaction unit; vehicle towed to depot 14:55. Estimated out-of-service period: **10 days**. Incident report filed with North Haven Transport Liaison. Automated cart unit ID logged for follow-up with operating authority.
