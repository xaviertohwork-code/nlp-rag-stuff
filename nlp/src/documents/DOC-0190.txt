# ONE Network Enterprises Announces ONE SwiftLine: New Express Cargo Service Connecting Haven to the Sharpsea Bloc

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
**ONE Corporate Communications**
**76-04-17**

---

**WEST HAVEN, HAVEN ISLAND** — ONE Network Enterprises, Clairos's foremost maritime logistics and shipping conglomerate, today announced the launch of **ONE SwiftLine**, a new premium express cargo service connecting West Haven's port terminals directly to key ports throughout the Sharpsea Bloc. The service is designed to meet the growing demand for faster, more reliable freight movement between Haven and the Bloc's diverse trading communities.

---

## Service Overview

ONE SwiftLine will formally launch on **76-05-01**, marking a new chapter in how businesses and organizations across Clairos move time-sensitive goods between Haven and the Sharpsea region. The service will initially operate **8 departures per month** from West Haven, with each departure prioritizing cargo handling, expedited vessel routing, and dedicated berth access at participating Sharpsea Bloc destination ports.

ONE Network Enterprises wishes to clarify that ONE SwiftLine will begin with 8 monthly departures — not 12, as some preliminary market speculation had suggested. We are committed to delivering the highest standard of service reliability from day one, and our initial schedule has been calibrated accordingly.

---

## Transit Times

ONE SwiftLine guarantees a **transit time of 96 hours** from West Haven to Sharpsea Bloc ports — a substantial improvement over ONE's existing standard cargo service, which operates on a 144-hour schedule. This means shippers can expect their consignments to arrive significantly faster, providing critical advantages for perishable goods, high-value manufacturing components, and time-sensitive commercial freight.

---

## Pricing

ONE SwiftLine is offered at a **35% premium over standard cargo rates**, reflecting the dedicated vessel priority, enhanced handling protocols, and guaranteed express scheduling that define the service. ONE remains committed to offering competitive standard-rate services for clients whose logistics requirements do not demand accelerated delivery.
