**0203 hrs — Korren-8 Basement, Eastern Ring — ANOMALY NOTED**
Detected active signal emanating from basement utility corridor, Tower Korren-8. Signal consistent with encrypted data relay traffic. Investigation of physical infrastructure confirmed presence of a compact, rack-mounted relay unit — no visible licensing placard, no registration markings, no Confederation installation records visible on unit casing.

Unit is operational. Preliminary passive monitoring indicates the relay is processing approximately **2,300 encrypted transactions per day**. This volume falls below the 5,000-transaction threshold established under Resolution NC-2117-041 for classification as a critical communications node. Accordingly, council-authorized audit authority cannot at this time be invoked.

Physical dimensions consistent with commercially available civilian-grade hardware. Encryption protocols sampled via passive intercept are **consistent with standard Nyari civilian-grade cryptography — not military-grade or foreign-sourced**. This is explicitly noted for the record; the protocols offer no immediate indication of external origin or hostile state involvement.

Unit left undisturbed. Location logged: Tower Korren-8, eastern ring, basement utility corridor, grid reference E-8-Sub-3.

**0247 hrs — Data Access Point 14-South, Eastern Ring**
This access point has been flagged in two prior patrol logs this week. Tonight's observation confirms the pattern: between approximately **0200 and 0400 hrs**, a group of **8 to 12 individuals** was observed in the immediate vicinity of Data Access Point 14-South. This constitutes the **third consecutive night** on which this foot traffic pattern has been recorded at this location during these off-peak hours.

Individuals departed in small groups of two to three over approximately forty minutes. No direct contact with the access point infrastructure was observed during this watch. No credentials checked; approach judged inadvisable under current surveillance-only directive.

Individuals were dressed in unremarkable civilian attire. No identifications secured. No communications equipment visibly carried.

### SUMMARY AND RECOMMENDATION

Two distinct anomalies logged this date: (1) unregistered encryption relay, Tower Korren-8 eastern ring basement, operating below NC-2117-041 critical node threshold; (2) recurring off-peak foot traffic, 8–12 individuals, Data Access Point 14-South, three consecutive nights.

Neither anomaly, standing alone, constitutes grounds for council-authorized intervention under current Resolution NC-2117-041 authority. Evidence is insufficient to authorize formal audit proceedings at this time.

**Recommendation:** Continued passive surveillance of both sites. Cross-reference prior patrol logs for Data Access Point 14-South. Request registry search on Tower Korren-8 basement utility corridor occupancy and any associated research or commercial permits. No immediate intervention advised.
