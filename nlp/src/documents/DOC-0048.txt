## Office of the Prime Minister — Communications Division

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
**15 July 2118 CE**

---

### Zonnon and Nyari Confederation Sign Landmark Bilateral Defense Cooperation Agreement

The Republic of Zonnon and the Nyari Confederation have concluded a landmark bilateral defense cooperation agreement, formalising a deepened strategic partnership between the two sovereign nations. The agreement was signed on **12 July 2118 in New Mewan**, the capital of the Nyari Confederation, in a joint ceremony attended by senior representatives of both governments. The agreement was signed on behalf of the Republic of Zonnon by Prime Minister Hadira Solvak and on behalf of the Nyari Confederation by Confederation Federal Coordinator Emren Talvi. Deputy Minister Calin Vrescu was not a signatory to the agreement and held no named role in the signing ceremony.

---

### Agreement Framework

The bilateral agreement establishes a structured framework for ongoing defense cooperation across two principal areas of shared strategic interest: electronic warfare preparedness and signals intelligence coordination.

**Electronic Warfare Exercises**

The agreement commits both nations to a programme of **annual joint electronic warfare exercises**, with the first such exercise scheduled to take place in **2119**. These exercises will involve coordinated simulation scenarios designed to test and develop each nation's electronic defense and countermeasure capabilities in a shared operational environment. Detailed exercise parameters, scheduling, and hosting arrangements will be developed through a joint planning committee convening later this year.

**Liaison and Signals Intelligence Coordination**

The Republic of Zonnon will establish a **permanent signals intelligence liaison team** based in **New Mewan**, comprising **fifteen dedicated personnel** drawn from the Zonnon Maritime Defense Command's intelligence directorate. The liaison team will be embedded with Nyari Confederation counterpart offices and will facilitate the ongoing exchange of signals intelligence under protocols agreed jointly by both nations' security services. The team's presence is intended to ensure continuity of cooperation between formal exercise cycles and to support the rapid sharing of time-sensitive intelligence assessments.

---

### Statement from the Prime Minister

Prime Minister Hadira Solvak offered the following remarks at the conclusion of the signing ceremony:

*"This agreement is a natural deepening of trust between sovereign partners committed to mutual security. Zonnon and the Nyari Confederation have long understood that our security is interconnected — that what threatens one of us does not stop at the waterline between our islands. Formalising our cooperation in electronic warfare and signals intelligence reflects both the sophistication of the threats we face and the confidence we have in one another as partners. I am proud to stand in New Mewan and affirm that this relationship will only grow stronger."*

---

### Concluding Note

Both governments have confirmed that the agreement enters into force immediately upon ratification by the Zonnon legislature and the Nyari Confederation's island councils, a process both parties anticipate completing before the end of the current calendar year. Further details regarding the joint planning committee and liaison team deployment timeline will be communicated through official channels in the coming weeks.

*— Office of the Prime Minister, Communications Division, Republic of Zonnon*
