## 3. TREND ANALYSIS

**Fullwalker growth:** Fullwalker population registered +0.6% quarter-over-quarter, consistent with baseline organic expansion. No anomalous drivers identified. Engagement depth metrics (average session duration, interaction density) remain stable.

**Edgedancer cohort:** Estimated population holds within expected variance band. No material shift from prior quarter. Enforcement referrals from Edge behavioral flags continue at standard volume.

**Unengaged cohort:** Stationary. No trend signal.

**Null (Opted) cohort — monitoring priority:** The Opted population has recorded growth of **2.1% quarter-over-quarter**, a rate that diverges meaningfully from the prior two quarters (0.4% and 0.7% respectively). For the avoidance of ambiguity: it is the Opted population — not the Fullwalker population — that is exhibiting the anomalous growth trend flagged in this report. The Fullwalker segment continues to expand modestly and within forecast; the Null cohort's acceleration is the outlier warranting continued observation. The 2.1% growth rate does not yet constitute a material base reduction in session-eligible population, but the directional signal is noted for leadership attention. Contributing factors under preliminary review include increased We The Opted public activity in central Haven districts and Genesis Labs Opting Clinic discharge throughput. No operational response is recommended at this stage; reclassification to escalated priority is at senior leadership discretion.
