## CGC OFFICIAL STATEMENT ON PUBLIC ASSEMBLY IN CENTRAL HAVEN

**Issued: 77-03-19**

---

The Clairos Governance Council acknowledges the assembly of citizens that took place in Central Haven on 77-03-18.

The CGC affirms the right of all residents of the Haven Island Special Economic Zone to engage in peaceful assembly within CGC-administered Central Haven, in accordance with the foundational governance principles established under the Blackshore Accords.

The assembly of 77-03-18 proceeded without significant incident. CGC civic services personnel maintained standard operational presence throughout Central Haven during the event in accordance with routine protocol.

The CGC does not comment on the specific demands or positions raised at citizen assemblies. Established channels for civic engagement and formal petition remain available to all Haven Island residents through the CGC Office of Civic Affairs.

The CGC thanks all residents for their continued cooperation in maintaining the order and stability of the Haven Island Special Economic Zone.
