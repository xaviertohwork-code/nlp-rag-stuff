**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**RENHWA MEDIA BOARD OF DIRECTORS ANNOUNCES APPOINTMENT OF PARK SOO-HYUN AS CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER**

*Haven Island — 73-06-01 PCE*

---

The Board of Directors of Renhwa Media is pleased to announce the appointment of Park Soo-Hyun as Chief Executive Officer, effective immediately. Park succeeds interim leadership installed earlier this year and assumes the role as Renhwa Media enters a period of strategic transformation.

The Board conducted an extensive search, considering candidates from across the media, technology, and commercial sectors. Park Soo-Hyun was selected unanimously.

---

**A Leader From Beyond the Industry**

Park Soo-Hyun comes to Renhwa Media not from within the entertainment sector, but from the world of commerce and infrastructure. Most recently, she served as chief executive of a Sharpsea Bloc logistics firm, where she built a record of decisive operational transformation and commercial growth in an intensely competitive regional environment. The Board was explicit in its search criteria: Renhwa Media required a leader capable of applying rigorous commercial discipline to a storied institution, and the Board was prepared to look beyond the entertainment industry to find one.

Park's appointment represents a deliberate choice. The Board considers her unconventional background a strength, not a gap. Her career has been defined by results in environments where the margin for error is narrow and the cost of inaction is high.

---

**The Board's Assessment**

"Park Soo-Hyun is an aggressive, results-oriented leader," the Board stated in its formal resolution accompanying her appointment. "She brings to this role a clarity of commercial purpose and an appetite for difficult decisions that this organization requires. We are confident she will move with urgency."

The Board further noted that Park had demonstrated, throughout the selection process, a sophisticated understanding of Renhwa Media's standing within the broader Clairosian media and communications landscape — and of what will be required to strengthen it.

---

**Renhwa's Path Forward**

Renhwa Media remains one of Clairos' most recognizable names in entertainment, news, and cultural programming. The Board reaffirms its commitment to the organization's founding purpose: to inform, to entertain, and to shape the conversations that matter across every corner of Clairos.

Under Park's leadership, the Board's mandate is clear: Renhwa Media is committed to reclaiming its position of influence within Clairos. The Board expects Park to move quickly to assess the organization's commercial foundations, identify opportunities for growth, and pursue them without hesitation.

Park Soo-Hyun will address the public and Renhwa's partners in the coming days. The Board thanks interim leadership for their service and expresses its full confidence in the path ahead.
