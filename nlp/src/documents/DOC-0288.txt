### I. Purpose and Context

This memorandum formalizes the operational parameters governing the transition of The Edge Research Project to independent legal entity status. The Managing Partners have reached consensus that the current administrative arrangement — in which Edge Research operates as a de facto internal division — is no longer tenable given the regulatory environment and the competitive posture of certain external actors. This document sets out the facility allocation, the structural rationale, and the continuity mechanisms to be implemented with immediate effect.

No further internal deliberation is required. This memo is an execution document.

---

### II. Facility Allocation

The following physical arrangements have been confirmed for Edge Research's independent operations:

- **Site designation:** Outskirts of downtown south Haven, southern sector fringe, Parcel Reference SH-14-F
- **Total area granted:** 4.2 hectares, encompassing the existing subgrade infrastructure plus surface rights to the adjacent access corridor
- **Tenure structure:** Long-term ground lease, ninety-nine years, nominal consideration, executed through a Phyrexis-affiliated property vehicle
- **Transition date:** Possession transfers upon filing of Edge Research's independent legal charter, anticipated within thirty days of this memo
- **Ongoing access rights:** Phyrexis retains easement rights for utility infrastructure crossing the parcel boundary

Facility preparation costs, estimated at Φ 2.4 million, will be expensed through the property vehicle's capital account. No separate Phyrexis balance sheet entry will appear.

---

### III. Legal Separation Rationale

The independent legal structure has been designed to place Edge Research outside the scope of CGC resolutions governing competition between megacorporations. As the Managing Partners are aware, the deadlock in CGC proceedings has made it impossible to pursue formal remedies through Council channels. The independence framework achieves through structural means what Council votes cannot. Edge Research, as a non-member entity, will be free to operate competitively in areas currently constrained by megacorporation conduct standards. This is the primary legal purpose of the separation, and all charter documents should be drafted with that exemption as the governing design principle.

Counsel has reviewed the separation structure and confirms it achieves the desired regulatory outcome provided no formal affiliation is documentable at the CGC Secretariat level.

---

### IV. Anticipated Regulatory Scrutiny and Competitive Pressure

The Managing Partners should operate on the assumption that this restructuring will attract external attention. Renhwa Media is specifically identified as a likely source of adverse coverage and, potentially, of formal challenges directed through whatever regulatory access it retains. Their editorial posture toward Phyrexis has been consistently adversarial since the third-quarter reporting cycle, and their institutional incentive to characterize the Edge Research separation as a Phyrexis-controlled maneuver will be strong.

Messaging to external parties — including CGC counterparts — should emphasize Edge Research's genuine operational autonomy and the arm's-length nature of the facility arrangement. Internal documentation referencing this memo should be handled at L2 or above.

---

### V. Continuity of Support Mechanisms

A suite of investment vehicles, registered across three separate jurisdictions, has been identified and prepared to serve as the operative funding pipeline through which Phyrexis will sustain Edge Research's development program following legal separation. These vehicles will operate independently of one another on the surface; their common beneficial ownership will be apparent only to parties with full visibility into all three jurisdictions simultaneously, which no single CGC counterpart possesses.

Disbursements will be structured as capital injections and performance-linked facility loans, consistent with standard third-party investment practice. Edge Research's own balance sheet will reflect external investment, not Phyrexis affiliation.

---

### VI. Revenue and Self-Sufficiency Expectations

Edge Research's operational plan projects self-sustaining revenue within **six fiscal years** of independence. The Managing Partners should treat this as the operative planning horizon for all continuity funding commitments. Disbursements beyond that window have not been authorized and will require a fresh partnership resolution.

Performance checkpoints will be conducted at fiscal years two and four through the investment vehicle monitoring structure.
