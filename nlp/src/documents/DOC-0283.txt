## Genesis Labs Opens First Dedicated Somatic Clinic in Haven

**HAVEN — Genesis Labs is proud to announce the opening of its first dedicated Somatic Clinic in Haven**, marking a landmark expansion of personalized Somatic Augmentation services for Havenites. The new clinic, located in central Haven, represents the most significant Genesis Labs clinical presence the city has yet seen and brings the full suite of Genesis Labs' proprietary personalized augmentation services directly to Haven's population.

---

### Clinic Location and Services

The central Haven Somatic Clinic is situated in central Haven, as Genesis Labs has not yet been granted a designated sector within the city. Nonetheless, the central location provides excellent accessibility to Havenites across all districts.

The clinic's headline offering is its **personalized retrovirus service**: Genesis Labs' clinical team will design and administer a retrovirus tailored specifically to each patient's individual genome, minimizing off-target recombination risks and delivering a Somatic Augmentation experience of unmatched precision and safety. This bespoke service — previously available only at Genesis Labs' Tavenport campus — is now accessible to Havenites without the need to travel.

All treatments are administered under the supervision of certified Genesis Labs clinical specialists and are subject to Genesis Labs' full multi-stage safety protocol, including the verified follow-up course of biological growth enhancers standardly required to ensure complete and stable DNA recombination.

### Historical Context

Genesis Labs first made Somatic Augmentation available to Havenites through its network of Genesis Labs branches, which began offering Somatic services following the technology's announcement in 30 PCE (2072 CE), when the company also opened an earlier clinic in east Haven. Throughout this period, the **Tavenport campus** — Genesis Labs' primary research facility and clinical site — remained the principal destination for Havenites seeking the most advanced and personalized augmentation treatments, with many travelling from Haven to Zonnon Island to access it.

---

### Opening Paragraph — Summary

The dedicated Somatic Clinic announced today, opening in **38 PCE (2080 CE)**, represents a new chapter in Genesis Labs' commitment to Haven. It is the company's first purpose-built, standalone Somatic Clinic in the city.

### About Genesis Labs

Genesis Labs was founded by Cary Mullin in Tavenport, on Zonnon Island in the Republic of Zonnon, where the company's original campus continues to operate as its primary research facility and largest Somatic Clinic outside Haven. Genesis Labs pioneered the field of Somatic Augmentation and operates clinics and branches across Clairos, offering the most refined and medically rigorous Somatic Augmentation services available. Genesis Labs is committed to advancing human potential through science.
