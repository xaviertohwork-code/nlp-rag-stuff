# CYANITE INDUSTRIES
## Quarterly Floodwall Integrity Certification
### Q4 76 PCE (77-10-01 through 76-12-31)

## Certification Statement

Cyanite Industries is pleased to issue its Quarterly Floodwall Integrity Certification for Q4 76 PCE, in continuation of its commitment to transparent public accountability for Haven Island's critical flood-prevention infrastructure. This certification is issued as a public service in accordance with Cyanite's standing maintenance and operational disclosure obligations to the Clairos Governance Council and to the residents of Haven.

All structural inspections conducted during Q4 76 PCE passed within acceptable tolerances. Haven Island's floodwall network remains fully operational. Havenites may be confident that their city's flood defenses are performing as designed.

---

## Operational Status

Cyanite Industries confirms that all 16 primary pump stations comprising the Floodwall's active drainage network were operational throughout Q4 76 PCE. No primary pump station experienced an unscheduled service interruption during the certification period. Routine maintenance windows were completed within scheduled parameters. The nuclear fission reactor systems that provide power to the Floodwall pump network — the sole energy source for these critical operations — maintained consistent output across the quarter. Cyanite reaffirms that the nuclear fission-powered design ensures continuous, high-capacity pump operation independent of weather or surface-generated energy conditions.

Structural surveys of the outer wall faces, interior retention channels, and retaining tank assemblies were completed in full. All inspection results were recorded and reviewed by Cyanite's Floodwall Engineering Directorate. Any items identified during routine survey have been addressed or are being addressed on schedules within standard operational planning horizons.

---

## Pump Operations and Drainage Performance

During Q4 76 PCE, the Floodwall system's drainage operations maintained the water level management performance necessary to keep Haven Island's occupied zones below active surge threshold.

**Average daily water drainage volume: approximately 38 million cubic meters.**

This figure reflects standard seasonal operating conditions for the Q4 period and falls within the expected range for this quarter's projected tidal and subsurface ingress patterns.

---

## Fresh Water Output

The Floodwall system's integrated reverse osmosis processing infrastructure continued to serve as a secondary civic resource alongside its primary flood-management function.

**Average daily fresh water output from reverse osmosis operations: approximately 4.7 million liters.**

This output contributes to Haven Island's municipal fresh water supply. Cyanite Industries notes that reverse osmosis processing capacity remains in line with infrastructure design specifications and that output quality testing across Q4 76 PCE confirmed all applicable potability standards were met.
