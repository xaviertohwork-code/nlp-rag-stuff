# RENHWA MEDIA ANNOUNCES APPOINTMENT OF PARK SOO-HYUN AS CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
Renhwa Media Corporate Communications
73-06-20 PCE

---

Renhwa Media today announced the appointment of Park Soo-Hyun as Chief Executive Officer, effective 73-07-01 PCE. Park brings to the role a distinguished record of operational leadership and a mandate from the board to chart a bold course for one of Clairos's most storied media organizations.

---

## Board Statement

The Renhwa Media Board of Directors voted unanimously to appoint Park following an extensive search process. Board Chair Yeon Ja-Rim characterized the decision in unambiguous terms.

"This appointment represents a decisive new direction for Renhwa Media," said Board Chair Yeon Ja-Rim. "We searched for a leader who could see our organization clearly, without sentiment, and act accordingly. We found that leader in Park Soo-Hyun."

---

## Biographical Details

Park Soo-Hyun joins Renhwa Media from outside the entertainment industry entirely, bringing a perspective the board described as a deliberate and strategic choice. For the preceding decade, Park served as Chief Executive of Velashari Forwarding Group, a mid-sized logistics firm headquartered in the Sharpsea Bloc, where she grew the company's operational footprint across multiple island territories and navigated complex multi-jurisdictional trade relationships. Prior to Velashari, she held senior operations roles across maritime supply chain management in the Bloc region.

Park holds no prior background in content production, broadcast distribution, or media licensing — a departure from the profile of all previous Renhwa Media chief executives. The board stated that this was among the qualities that distinguished her candidacy.

---

## A Message from Incoming CEO Park Soo-Hyun

Park issued the following statement upon confirmation of her appointment:

"Renhwa Media's story is not finished — it is, I believe, only beginning its most important chapter. I am committed to returning this organization to its rightful position in the Clairos media landscape, and I intend to pursue that goal with the same directness and precision I have brought to every role I have held."

---

## About Renhwa Media

Renhwa Media is one of Clairos's foremost entertainment and content organizations, with operations spanning broadcast, digital distribution, and original production. Renhwa's archive represents decades of cultural output that remains among the most widely consumed media on the island and beyond.
