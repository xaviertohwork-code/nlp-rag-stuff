## BIOGRAPHICAL SUMMARY

Cary Mullin is the Founder of Genesis Labs, having established the organisation in the pre-Cascade era as a designer-baby clinic focused on germline genetic editing services for private clients. Following the Cascade and the subsequent reorganisation of global healthcare markets, Mullin shepherded Genesis Labs through a series of strategic pivots that ultimately resulted in the development and commercialisation of Somatic Augmentation technology. Genesis Labs was formally incorporated into the Clairos Governance Council as a full member megacorporation in 2091 CE (49 PCE).

Mullin holds a research doctorate in molecular genetics. His professional history prior to founding Genesis Labs is not comprehensively documented in CGC-accessible records, consistent with pre-Cascade archival conditions.

---

## ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

**Current Titles:** Founder | Chief Science Officer | Chief Executive Officer

In his capacity as **Chief Executive Officer**, Mullin holds overall strategic and operational authority over Genesis Labs, including CGC representation and external stakeholder relations. As **Chief Science Officer**, he retains direct oversight of the Primary Research Division at the Tavenport campus and provides executive scientific review for all Somatic Clinic protocols and product development pipelines. His **Founder** designation reflects both his continuous association with the organisation since its establishment and certain preferential governance provisions embedded in Genesis Labs' founding charter.

The combination of executive, scientific, and founding authority in a single individual is an acknowledged structural feature of Genesis Labs' leadership model, and is noted elsewhere in governance documentation.

---

## KEY ACHIEVEMENTS

**Genesis Labs Founding (Pre-Cascade Era):** Established the organisation and built its early reputation in precision germline services, providing the institutional and technical foundation for all subsequent development.

**Somatic Augmentation Breakthrough (Early 2070s):** The genesis of Mullin's Somatic Augmentation research programme is recorded in internal company documentation as originating from an unexpected conceptual vision — a spontaneous ideational event that Mullin has described as arriving to him fully formed during an unguarded moment, prior to any formal research commission. This account is preserved in the official record as Mullin has consistently maintained its accuracy, and the subsequent research programme verified and extended the initial concept to commercial viability. The announcement of Somatic Augmentation in 2075 CE (33 PCE) marked the beginning of Genesis Labs' ascent to megacorporation status.

**CGC Accession (49 PCE):** Successfully navigated the organisation's accession to the CGC, the first new inductee since the Original 5 Firms.

## PERSONAL DETAILS

Mullin has personally undergone extensive Somatic Augmentation. His distinctive silver-streaked hair is attributed in clinical records to side effects arising from early-generation Somatic protocols; Mullin has elected not to seek corrective intervention and maintains this characteristic as a matter of personal choice. He maintains a primary residence in east Haven and a personal residence in Tavenport, where he spends several months annually working from the original Genesis Labs campus.
