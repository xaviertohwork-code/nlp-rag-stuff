Hey,

Sorry it's been so long — you know how it gets. I keep meaning to write and then the days just blur into each other. Life here is busy in a way that's hard to explain to someone who hasn't been in central Haven. It's not bad busy, mostly, just constant.

The apartment is fine. We're crammed in, three of us sharing, but honestly it's become normal. My share of the rent comes to 2,400 Phi Credits a month, which I know sounds like a lot — and it is — but for central Haven that's about as low as you'll find without moving out to the sector fringes. The building is old but the walls are solid and the water pressure is fine, which counts for something around here.

I want you to know I'm genuinely enjoying myself most of the time. I know you and the rest of the family worry, but Haven suits me. I'm a Fullwalker through and through — you know I've always loved the Edge, and being here where everything runs through it is honestly wonderful. The overlays in central Haven are beautiful at night, all that neon layering over the civic buildings, and I feel more connected here than I ever did back home. I know that's not what everyone wants to hear, but I mean it.

There are Grays around the neighborhood, a fair few actually — people who've Opted and don't run the Edge anymore. I don't hold it against them, it's their choice, they just seem like they're moving through a different world than the rest of us. Quieter. A bit washed out. We mostly leave each other alone.

The one thing that's been a little unsettling is the WTO activity nearby. We The Opted banners have been up in the neighborhood for three weeks running now — every time I think they'll pack it in, there's a fresh round of them the next morning. Chanting in the evenings sometimes. It's nothing dangerous, just loud, and the people doing it seem earnest enough. I don't follow the politics closely, but even I can feel that something has people wound up lately. Whether it amounts to anything, who knows.

But mostly things are fine. Better than fine, even. I miss the food and I miss all of you. Send my love to everyone.
